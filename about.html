About Aggie Cuts

Aggie Cuts is a student-centered barbershop founded with one goal in mind: to provide high-quality, affordable haircuts right on campus. We understand the busy lifestyle of college students and offer convenient services to keep you looking sharp without leaving school grounds.

Whether you're preparing for a job interview, a date, or just need a fresh look for the week, our experienced barbers deliver clean fades, trims, and shaves tailored to your style.
